Summer ICT Training

Every summer, most kids and teens are on holiday, after successfully completing another academic year. Parents always want their children to be productive and engaged during these holidays. Gateway Computers organizes a special ICT Training programme, with these kids and teens in mind. The training is aimed at equipping children with the appropriate ICT skills , giving them a strong start in ICT and its related courses. This training will not just help them in their higher education, but will also be an added advantage that will broaden the choices they make in life. With interactive class sessions, educational games, excursions and team activities, they will have the opportunity to learn new things while having fun!

Our Summer Programs

The Programs for the Summer ICT Training are similar to our regular courses but has been streamlined or minified to accomodate the level of learning of the kids and teenagers. Advanced courses such as Coding, Graphics Design, Microsoft Excel, & Web Design have been simplified so that the kids can easily grasp their concepts. Click here to apply now.

IFEPS - Our Premium Partner

Gateway Computers has partnered with the Institute For Ethics and Professional Studies (IFEPS) to provide a special course to the kids and teens that are participating in the Summer ICT Training. This course specializes on the character development, public speaking, people interaction, and career goals of the students. Arrangements are been made for all students to participate in the course. Also available to the students are Language classes; French, German and Spanish.
