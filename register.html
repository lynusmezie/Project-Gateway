Training Terms & Guidelines

  1. I shall pay the training fees as agreed and scheduled.
  2. Decision to discontinue with my course of study will not lead to refund of fees paid.
  3. Failure to pay the training fees as agreed and scheduled will disqualify me from attending classes.
  4. I shall pay for any equipment damaged by me during the training.
  5. The training center is not avenue for me to entertain visitors.
  6. Changing of lecture time after three (3) weeks is NOT allowed.
  7. All complains should be directed to the Customer Service Unit.
  8. Urgent/ express collection of Certificate/Diploma attracts a payment of N1,000.00.
  9. Noise making in the computer lab is not allowed.
  10. Making/Answering of phone calls in not permitted inside the computer lab.
  11. Insult and assault of any staff attracts punishment as deemed fit by the authority.
  12. No student should sit on a staff seat for any reason.
  13. Fighting, stealing, examination malpractice attracts instant dismissal without refund of school fees.
  14. Charging of handsets and other devices in the computer lab is at owner’s risk.
  15. Eating and drinking in the lab is prohibited.
  16. The training center will not repeat lessons missed by me.
  17. The training center is not a custodian of students’ money or valuables.
  18. Indecent dressing is not allowed in the premises.
  19. Headphone/Earpiece/Speakers and cameras are not allowed in the training premises.
  20. Collection of transcript and Certificate/Diploma attracts a payment of N500.00..
