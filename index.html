About Us

Founded in 2002 and headquartered in Beirut Road, Kano, Nigeria, Gateway Computers was named The Third Most Effective Computer Training Centre in Kano State by CPN in the year 2005. In 2006, the company moved some of its operations to the nation's capital, establishing new branches at strategic locations around the Federal Capital Territory: First, in the bustling satellite town of Kubwa, subsequently in the ever-growing town of Bwari and most recently, in Gwarinpa, one of the largest estate in West Africa. Other branches have been established in two other locations, Kano and Ekiti states.

Our Vision

The company's vision is aimed at reducing the level of computer illiteracy in Nigeria, by enhancing the computer proficiency and IT Skills of organizations or individuals and to be one of the most reliable provider of IT Skills training in Nigeria.
